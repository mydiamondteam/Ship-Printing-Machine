PROJECT GUIDE

The Meteorite Space Port Machine Token - (Coming Soon!)
Official Launch Date: 03/25/22 - Tentative!

The value of MTR token will rise as more users invest SPO/BNB LP into the project.

1. Stake SPO/BNB LP and earn Daily Staking Rewards in MTR token (3.30% per day).You can claim your MTR rewards anytime. You cannot unstake your staked SPO/BNB LP .
• You will earn MTR tokens as a percentage of SPO/BNB LP invested irrespective of the MTR Price.
• i.e. If you invest 1 SPO/BNB LP you will receive 0.033 MTR daily. • Investors have two options with their MTR tokens, they can sell them for SPO/BNB LP , or you can stake them to earn more MTR tokens (5.50% per day). MTR staking is locked for 7 days.

2.Project Terminology
• Total Supply : The maximum amount of MTR that can exist.
• Circulating Supply : The amount of MTR tokens that are currently in the investors wallets.
• Available Suppy : (Total Supply - Cirulating Supply).
• MTR Price : (Total SPO/BNB LP Balance / Available Supply).

3. Mint MTR - As you claim MTR from minting, it is removed from the Available Supply and added to the Circulating Supply.

4. Sell MTR - As you sell MTR , it is removed from the Circulating Supply and added to the Available Supply.

5. Investors can also choose to sell their MTR tokens for profit or hold on to it since it will have a usecase for layer 3. Visit our telegram group to see our roadmap!
